Activity 2 How do bacteria in yogurt look like? Objectives:

After performing this activity, you should be able to:

1. identify bacteria present in fermented food or drink
2. describe bacteria observed under the microscope
3. explain the use of bacteria in food or drink making

Materials Needed:

2 droppers
coverslip
microscope
diluted yogurt
glass slide
methylene blue

Procedure:

1. Place a drop of diluted yogurt on a slide.
2. Add a drop of methylene blue and cover with a cover slip.
